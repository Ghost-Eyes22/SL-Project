One Piece Is

A manga series written and illustrated in Japanese by Eiichiro Oda. The series has been serialized in Shueisha's shōnen manga magazine Weekly Shōnen Jump since July 1997. With the chapters compiled in 109 volumes as of July 2024 The plot of the anime One Piece is about the main character Monkey D. Luffy and his crew of pirates searching the seas known as the north blue, east blue, south blue, west blue, the grand line, and the new world. Luffy and his crew search these seas for the legendary treasure known as the “One Piece” which Luffy believes will make him the pirate king.

The Story

Takes place in an alternate version of Earth where the age known as The Golden Age of Pirates occurs. The previous Pirate King, Gol D. Roger, left behind the treasure known as “The One-piece” before being executed by the world government (mostly populated by the Marines). Luffy was only 7 years old when he ate the “human-human” model: Nika, or the “gum-gum” fruit. The Gum-Gum fruit gives Luffy the power to stretch like rubber which he uses to his benefit while going out on his adventure to find the One Piece and be king of the pirates.

The Crew

However, Luffy wasnt alone on his journey. He had a crew that he picked out specifically during his trip. He called his crew the Straw Hat Pirates, which includes Roronoa Zoro, a swordsman who used to be a bounty hunter, Nami, their navigator who used to steal from pirates, Usopp, Their sniper and (to this day) a compulsive liar, Sanji, their cook and fighter, who used to be a cook at the Baratie, a floating restaurant in the East Blue, Tony Tony Chopper, Their doctor, Nico Robin, Their archeologist, Franky, their mechanic, and Jimbei, another fighter. While on this Journey Luffy and his crew face rivals that last till the end of the series or multiple arcs (chapters) while outpacing the Marines as they search the sea for the one piece. Their first ship was the Going Merry which burned to flames but was then replaced by the Thousand Sunny thanks to Franky, Their mechanic.

The Theme

The series' central themes are exemplified by Monkey D. Luffy, who is a strong advocate for freedom and independence. One piece mostly explores the themes regarding freedom, independence, loyalty, and resolve. In the anime, these themes mean:
  • Loyalty: The Straw Hat Pirates logo embodies Luffy's ideals and values, including loyalty.
  • Resolve: The Straw Hat Pirates' logo embodies Luffy's ideals and values, including an unwavering resolve to protect his friends.
  • Freedom: Luffy is a strong advocate for freedom and independence and is determined to follow his own rules and path, even if it means going against society.
